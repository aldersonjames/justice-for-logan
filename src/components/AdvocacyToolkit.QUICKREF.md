## Key Statistics to Remember

- **30%** of violent crime defendants are rearrested on pretrial release
- **25%** of defendants charged with violent crimes commit new offenses while on bail
- **1,000+** preventable deaths annually from crimes by defendants out on bond
- **July 7, 2024** - Logan Holley, age 20, killed by repeat DUI offender out on bond

## 5 Core Policy Asks

1. **Mandatory risk assessments** for violent crime defendants
2. **Enhanced monitoring** (GPS tracking) for high-risk defendants
3. **Victim notification** when defendants are released
4. **Judicial accountability** review mechanisms
5. **Public transparency** through annual reporting
